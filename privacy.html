Privacy Policy

// WHAT WE COLLECT

When you scan a rejection we store: the job title, verdict, risk level, and EEOC category. We do NOT store the full text of your rejection. When you provide your email with explicit consent, we store it linked to your scan ID.

// WHAT WE DO NOT COLLECT

We do not collect your name, address, phone number, or any personally identifying information. All scans are anonymous by default.

// HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

Scan data is used to improve pattern detection accuracy and generate anonymous aggregate statistics. Email addresses are used only to send updates about employment rights — never sold, never shared with third parties.

// YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to request deletion of your data at any time. Contact us and we will delete your records within 48 hours.

// GDPR AND CCPA

We comply with GDPR (EU) and CCPA (California) regulations. You provided explicit consent before submitting your email. You may withdraw consent at any time.

// LEGAL DISCLAIMER

BiasLens is not a law firm. Results are pattern analysis only and do not constitute legal advice. Always consult a qualified employment attorney before taking legal action.
